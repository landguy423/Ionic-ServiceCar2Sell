What Size Vehicle?

STANDARD

All passenger cars, compact trucks and small to medium SUV's. (Examples: Honda Accord, Toyota Tacoma, Audi Q5, Ford Escape, Jeep Liberty)

LARGE

Full size trucks, large SUV's, vans and mini vans. (Examples: Ford F150, Chevy Tahoe, Honda odyssey, Audi Q7, Ford Explorer, Jeep Wrangler (4rd))
